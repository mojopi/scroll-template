THE MIND READER

The Mind Reader...

..is a device meant to help you reflect on your inner dialogue, helping you to be mindful as you meditate. It works by providing an audio experience — a meditative guide — featuring carefully selected sound effects and verbal affirmations and visualizations. It all provides a visual focal point of turning gears and words that can help you externalize anxiety about your thoughts and instead bring about inner piece.

It was made using real impressions and moulds of our faces along with laser cut gears and hand-selected words. The color choices and painting on the gears are meant to bring about a trans-like state that induces focus and thus, inner serenity.
